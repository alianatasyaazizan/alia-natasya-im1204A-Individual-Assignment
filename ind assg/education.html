EDUCATION


SJK(C) SAN YUK


From the age of 7 to 12 I have been attended primary school at SJK(C) San Yuk. Is anyone in my family chinese?? That's the answer that I always got because I went to school in SJK(C). However, I was actually schooled in a Chinese school because when I was in kindergarten I went to a Chinese language school which when I entered primary school, I was suggested by one of my kindergarten teachers to persue my third language in Chinese when entering grade 1 because I was considered fluent and smart. Alhamdulillah, I learned a lot about racism and religion of other races and I also survived to go to school in SJK (C) even though I am a Malay.



SMK TUN PERAK, RAWANG


After 8 years of learning Chinese in a Chinese school, I feel tired and want to start a Malay school. Therefore, I attended high school a malay highschool called SMK Tun Perak. Many things I learned for the first time because for the first time I had so many Malay students around me. I was a bit culture shocked at first but I SURVIVEDD AGAIN!!! From this school I started to build my identity and have many new friends until now.
